A. Bottom tab navigation

Feels app-like and keeps primary pages one tap away. Weak fit for brand dashboard because it has more than five destinations.

B: Top Bar + Drawer

B. Top bar with slide-out menu

Best balance. Desktop keeps the sidebar. Mobile gets a compact header, drawer menu, and full-width content.

C: Horizontal Scroll Nav

C. Horizontal pill nav

Smallest implementation, but weak once there are many pages. Users may miss hidden nav items on small phones.

Recommendation

  • Use option B: responsive top bar plus drawer on mobile, keep sidebar on desktop.

Scope guard

  • Fix shell, spacing, cards, and table presentation first. Avoid redesigning business logic.
